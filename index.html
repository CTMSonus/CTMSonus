General Operating Description

For the patient:

Sonus is an application that allows sound stimulation during sleep for the treatment of Tinnitus.

For the clinic:

Sonus is an application that allows individualized sound design for your patients to be stimulated with sound during sleep for the treatment of Tinnitus. It allows patient management, including tracking and collection and visualization of clinical data throughout the treatment.

Compatible Hardware
Patients:
Needs iOS 15 or iPadOS 15 
Technical/ Backend:
Needs iOS 16 or iPadOS 16, MacOS Ventura.

Currently needs to install via testflight

Terms to be set:

RAI: 
Individualized Acoustic Prescription: is the set of sounds set by a CTM specialist that recreate the patient's tinnitus. This sound is the one used by the patient to stimulate him/herself.

Generator: code in charge of emitting and manipulating 1 RAI sound. 
There are 4 types of generators
